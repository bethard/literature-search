Train a Literature Search Model

The first step in training a model is to select a set of articles that demonstrate good citation habits for your field. Your goal is to find articles that are representative of your area of research, and more importantly, have excellent bibliographies. The system learns its literature search model by comparing an article's contents against each of the articles in its bibliography.

1. Search for articles

We'll assume that you know the titles for a number of appropriate articles. Please use the search below to find your articles in the index. When you find an article, click the "Use for training" button beside it to add it to the training data. If you make a mistake, you can click the "Remove from training" button beside an article to remove it from the training data.
